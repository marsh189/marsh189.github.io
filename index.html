About Me

I am currently enrolled at Chapman Universirty where I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with a minor in Game Development Programming. I will receive my degree in May 2018. I also work for Chapman University's IS&T department as a Web Development Assistant. As a Web Development Assistant, I have the task of improving and maintaining the schools web and mobile applications using ASP.NET and C#.

Throughout my studies, the aspects of game development I enjoy working on the most are the User Interface and triggered effects. As a gamer, I have seen games that do not have an interface that is up to par with the game, so while I make my own games, I make sure the menus look just as nice and work jsut as well as the rest of the game. I also love making the player trigger certain things. This includes the player killing an enemy with a gun, picking up an item, or falling to their deaths. Most of my game developing experience has bee with Unity, although I have some experience using Unreal nd their blueprint system.
